About Me

Hi there! I'm Leonard. I am passionate about user-centred design because it helps us empathise deeply with our users and drive thoughtful trade-offs & decisions, to create meaningful products that deliver value for the people who use them.

I enjoy working with product & engineering leaders to tackle a common problem using our unique perspectives and strengths. Together, we can leave the world a little better than we found it.

If you'd like to collaborate, don't hesitate to reach out at chen.junhuo@gmail.com
