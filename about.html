About


I believe design is how we can make impact on the public good at scale

I'm a user experience designer, with a focus on user research, product design, and product strategy, working for the Singapore Government.

My journey in design began with studying the psychology of how people decide to help their close friends and random strangers. I then progressed to understanding how communities interact and make joint decisions on their community budget and allocation for public services.

Designing for the public good is the perfect intersection of my passions in psychology and the community.

For fun, I practice capoeira, go on long runs, and hunt down a good cup of kopi-c siu dai. Feel free to learn more about me and my thoughts on Medium and Instagram!
