Addressing information asymmetry

We heard many drivers say that the majority of appeals are accepted - when in fact the majority of appeals are rejected. This was probably because many successful appellants share their 'success' stories, while those whose appeals are rejected do not go around broadcasting their 'failures'.

We wanted to make sure that potential appellants knew - before they even began the appeal process - that most appeals are rejected. We included many opportunities for these users to choose to pay their parking fine instead.
